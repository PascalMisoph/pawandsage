# Instagram Content Plan — Week 1 (Launch Week)

**Goal:** Establish brand presence, collect first 100–300 followers, get IG algorithm to classify @pawandsage as a "calm cat behavior / indoor cat lifestyle" account so future Reels reach the right audience.

**Strategy:** Launch with assets already produced (Welcome image, 2 Dr. Sage videos, 1 carousel). Fill gaps with three lightweight, low-production posts (mood quote, story poll, Sunday reset) so production load in Week 1 stays near zero.

**Posting time (locked for Week 1):** 18:00 CET — peak EU evening + start of US east-coast lunch break (overlap window for the bilingual indoor-cat audience).

## Story rhythm summary (separate from grid)

Every grid post gets a story re-share within 1 hour — non-negotiable, doubles reach.
Beyond that, daily stories should hit at least one of:
- **Behind-the-scenes** (writing on the laptop, MJ generation in progress, voice-recording session)
- **Engagement sticker** (poll, question, quiz, slider)
- **UGC repost**
- **Saved Highlight maintenance** (build out: `Welcome`, `Behavior`, `Enrichment`, `Cozy`)

Aim 3–5 stories per day across Week 1. Don't spam — quality over volume.
