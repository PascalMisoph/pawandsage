## Break-Even for Quitting WFS
- Minimum: €3,500-4,000 net/month (stable over 6+ months)
- Comfortable: €5,000+ net/month with 3-6 months runway
- Safety buffer: €15,000-20,000 in savings at point of resignation
- Realistic quit point: Age 31-33 (2028-2030)

## Decision Rules
1. No new app before CatPlay has 100 paying users
2. No new feature that doesn't directly improve retention or conversion
3. No paid ads before organic funnel is validated
4. Every Sunday: 30 min KPI review
5. Kill criterion: If CatPlay has <50 paying users after 6 months → pivot niche
