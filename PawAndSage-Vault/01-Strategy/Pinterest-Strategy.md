## Daily Posting Rhythm (~15 min/day)
- **Mon**: New pin (Article of the week) → main board + 5 foreign repins
- **Tue**: 2nd pin variant same article → different board + 3 foreign repins
- **Wed**: Redistribute older own pin + 5 foreign repins
- **Thu**: 3rd pin variant → post + 3 foreign repins
- **Fri**: New article publish + 1st pin + 3 foreign repins
- **Sat**: Remaining pins for article 2 + redistribute older pins
- **Sun**: Light — 5 foreign repins only

## Pin Ratio
- Months 1-3: 30% own pins, 70% foreign repins
- Months 4+: 50/50

## Pin Format
- Size: 1000×1500px (2:3 ratio)
- 4 pins per article (Problem, Listicle, Emotional, Product)
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headlines) + Nunito Sans (body)
- Colors: Sage green (#4D7C5A), Gold (#A68832), Cream (#FBF8F3), Charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- Always include "pawandsage.com" on pin
- All pins link to the article URL
