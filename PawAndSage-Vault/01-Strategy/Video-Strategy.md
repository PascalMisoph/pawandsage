# Short-Form Video Strategy (from Month 3 / July 2026)

## Format
Faceless cat content. No face needed. Recipe:
- Stock cat video (Pexels/Pixabay) as background
- Bold text overlay with hook + key points
- Optional voiceover (or text-only)
- 30-60 seconds per video
- Every video based on an existing blog article

## Platforms
TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts — same video, all three platforms, auto-scheduled via Metricool.

## Posting Rhythm
5 videos/week, Monday through Friday, on all 3 platforms simultaneously.

## Video Types
1. **Explainer** — "The REAL reason your cat does X"
2. **Listicle** — "5 things your indoor cat NEEDS"
3. **Product Push** — "This $8 toy changed everything" (affiliate)
4. **Quick Tip** — "Try this next time your cat..."
5. **Myth Bust** — "Stop believing this about cats"

## Hook Pattern (first 3 seconds)
Every video opens with a provocative or curiosity-driven statement. No intros, no logos, no "hey guys." Straight into the hook.

## CTA Pattern (last 5 seconds)
"Full guide + product picks → link in bio" or "More tips → link in bio"

## Production: Saturday Batch (2-3 hours)
1. Pick 5 articles → write hooks (15 min)
2. Find stock videos on Pexels (20 min)
3. Build in CapCut — duplicate template, swap text + clip (60-90 min)
4. Write captions + hashtags (15 min)
5. Upload to Metricool, schedule Mon-Fri (15 min)

## Tool Stack
| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| CapCut Mobile | Video editing, text overlays | Free |
| Metricool | Auto-scheduling TikTok + IG + YT | Free (50 posts/mo) |
| Pexels/Pixabay | Stock cat videos | Free |

## Videos Per Article
Each article produces 2-3 videos:
- Video A: Explainer (why/what)
- Video B: Solution/Tips (how to fix)
- Video C: Product spotlight (affiliate push)
