# Content Strategy — Paw & Sage

## Niche
Cat behavior & care for indoor cat owners. English-language, global audience (US/UK/AU primary).

## Content Categories (rotate endlessly)
1. **"Why Does My Cat..." (Behavior Explainer)** — Explains weird cat behaviors. High search volume, medium affiliate potential. Examples: knocking things off, staring, biting when petted.
2. **"Best X for Y" (Product Roundup)** — Classic affiliate content. Highest purchase intent, highest commission. Examples: best puzzle feeders, best cat trees.
3. **"How to Stop Your Cat From..." (Solution Guide)** — Problem-solution format. High search volume, good affiliate fit. Examples: scratching furniture, meowing at night.
4. **"X vs Y" (Comparison)** — Comparison articles rank well, natural product embeds. Examples: wet vs dry food, laser vs wand toy.
5. **"Signs Your Cat Is..." (Awareness/Concern)** — Emotional content, great for Pinterest. Examples: signs of boredom, stress, loneliness.

## Article Format
- Length: 800-1,200 words
- Structure: Hook (2-3 sentences) → Numbered sections (H2s) → Each section ends with product recommendation → Bottom CTA (CatPlay cross-sell + email opt-in)
- Tone: Warm, knowledgeable friend. Never preachy or guilt-trippy.
- Affiliate links: 3-5 per article, contextually embedded (not listy)
- Internal links: Link to 2-3 other Paw & Sage articles where relevant

## Publishing Cadence
- 2 articles per week
- Tuesday: Article 1 publish
- Friday/Saturday: Article 2 publish

## SEO Approach
- Target long-tail question keywords ("why does my cat...")
- Each article targets ONE primary keyword
- URL format: /primary-keyword-slug/
- Meta description: ~155 chars, includes primary keyword
- Add FAQ schema where applicable
