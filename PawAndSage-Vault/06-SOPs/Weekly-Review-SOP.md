# SOP: Sunday Weekly Review (30 min)

## Step 1: Numbers (10 min)
- Check blog analytics (pageviews, top articles)
- Check Pinterest analytics (impressions, clicks, saves)
- Check affiliate dashboard (clicks, conversions, revenue)
- Update KPIs.md with this week's numbers

## Step 2: Reflect (10 min)
- Open Weekly-Review.md, copy template, fill in:
  - What got done?
  - What didn't get done? Why?
  - Energy level?
  - Key learnings?

## Step 3: Plan Next Week (10 min)
- Which 2 articles are next? (check Content Plan)
- Any Pinterest pins still to post?
- Any video batch needed? (from Month 3)
- Set Top 3 priorities in Home.md
- Done by noon. Rest of Sunday is FREE.
