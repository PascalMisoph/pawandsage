# SOP: Writing a Paw & Sage Article

## Before Writing
1. Check Content Plan for the next article number and topic
2. Note the primary keyword, secondary keywords, and affiliate products
3. Check Article Index for internal linking opportunities

## Writing (Claude Code shortcut: "Artikel X")
1. Open the Content Plan, find article #X
2. Write 800-1,200 words following this structure:
   - **Hook** (2-3 sentences, relatable problem)
   - **H2 sections** (numbered, each addressing one reason/tip/point)
   - **Each section ends** with a product recommendation: `[AFFILIATE: Product ($price)]`
   - **Bottom CTA**: CatPlay cross-sell + email opt-in mention
3. Include internal links to 2-3 published articles
4. Save as `02-Articles/drafts/XX-slug-name.md`

## After Writing
1. Replace `[AFFILIATE: ...]` placeholders with real Amazon/Chewy links
2. Add to Astro blog, set frontmatter (title, description, pubDate, tags)
3. Publish and submit URL to Google Search Console
4. Move file from `drafts/` to `published/`
5. Update `Article-Index.md`
6. Create 4 Pinterest pins (see Pinterest Posting SOP)
