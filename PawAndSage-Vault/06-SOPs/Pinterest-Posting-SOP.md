# SOP: Pinterest Posting

## For Each New Article (4 pins)
1. Create 4 pin designs in Canva (1000×1500px):
   - Pin 1: Problem pin (emotional hook)
   - Pin 2: Listicle pin (numbered tips)
   - Pin 3: Emotional/curiosity pin
   - Pin 4: Product pin (specific price = high CTR)
2. Use Paw & Sage brand colors and fonts (Playfair Display + Nunito Sans)
3. Upload to Pinterest with:
   - **Title**: Include primary keyword
   - **Description**: 2-3 sentences with keywords, natural language
   - **Link**: Article URL on pawandsage.com
4. Schedule over 4 days (1 pin per day, different boards)
5. Update Pin-Tracker.md

## Daily Routine (~15 min)
1. Post 1 scheduled pin (own content)
2. Repin 3-5 relevant pins from other cat accounts
3. Check notifications, respond to comments
