# Brand Voice — Paw & Sage

## Tone
**Warm, knowledgeable friend who happens to be a cat expert.**

### We ARE:
- Friendly and approachable
- Science-informed but not clinical
- Practical — every article ends with actionable advice
- Honest about products (we recommend what works, not what pays most)
- Slightly playful, never silly

### We are NOT:
- Preachy ("as a responsible cat parent you SHOULD...")
- Guilt-trippy ("your cat is suffering because you don't...")
- Clickbaity ("you won't BELIEVE what happened!")
- Generic AI-sounding ("in this comprehensive guide, we will explore...")
- Overly casual ("yo cat parents what's up")

## Writing Rules
1. Lead with the relatable moment, not the explanation
2. Use "you" and "your cat" — never "one" or "the cat owner"
3. Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
4. Bold the key takeaway in each section
5. End every section with a concrete next step or product recommendation
6. No filler sentences. Every sentence earns its place.

## Example Hooks
✅ "You're sitting at your desk when you hear it — the slow scrape of a glass being nudged toward the edge."
❌ "Have you ever wondered why your feline companion engages in the curious behavior of pushing objects off surfaces?"

## CTA Style
✅ "Struggling to keep track of your cat's play habits? We're building CatPlay — a simple app that helps you build a daily routine."
❌ "DOWNLOAD OUR APP NOW! Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to transform your cat's life!"
