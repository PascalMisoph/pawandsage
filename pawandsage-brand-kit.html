05 — Voice & Tone

How paw and sage speaks.

Calm, observant, useful. Like a friend who has lived with cats long enough to notice things — and won't dress them up.

✓ We sound like
  • Quietly observant
  • Specific over general
  • Useful in the first sentence
  • Warm without being cute
  • Confident, not loud

"A cat who finishes a bowl in thirty seconds and then stares at you is bored, not hungry."

✗ We avoid
  • "Furbabies" and "purr-fect"
  • Cliché cat puns in headlines
  • Excessive emojis 🐾🐱✨
  • Guilt-marketing ("good cat parents…")
  • Generic listicle padding

"10 PURR-FECT toys your furbaby will go CRAZY for! 🐾🐱"
